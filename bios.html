Meet the Band

Kevin McNamara

Kevin McNamara is the lead guitarist of the band and has been melting the faces of Charlestonians for over two decades with his jaw-dropping solos!

David Flenner

From Kurt Cobain, to Zach De La Rocha, to Olivia Rodrigo, Dave dazzles the audience with exciting vocals across many genres. Having over 25 years of experience with the acoustic guitar, Dave also backs up his singing with the rhythm guitar in the band.


Dave started playing in high school and was a former member of The Leftorium - an acousting duo with Kevin McNamara. The Leftorium played shows around Charleston for about 7 years at great venues such as: Sesame Burgers and Brew (multiple locations), Wine Down, and Coastal Coffee Roasters.


These days he is venturing out from the acoustic and has explored with electric - you'll find him changing instruments throughout the shows.


Justin Sellers

Our newest member of the band - Justin has found a home in deep in the rhythm section with his heart pounding bass. Just wait until you hear him rocking out tunes by The Red Hot Chili Peppers, Cake, and Pink Floyd!





Jason Gillespie

The heart of the band is Jason, who keeps everyone else in perfect time. Jason also has a lot of experience playing with local bands such as the Rescue Blues!
