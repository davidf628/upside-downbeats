Welcome to the Official Website of the Upside Downbeats!

Discover our music, meet the band, and explore our journey. Check out photos, videos, and learn more about us!

We are a four-piece local rock band with an extensive cover selection including artists like: Cake, The Killers, Rage Against the Machine, The Cranberries, Wilco, Pink Floyd, Tears for Fears, and many, many more!

We also have a short list of great originals written by our lead guitarist Kevin McNamara!

Together, we have a long history of rocking out the Charleston area featuring great spots such as: The Celt, Carolina Butcher and Beer Garden, The Commonhouse, and more!

Let us know how we can help rock out your next show!
